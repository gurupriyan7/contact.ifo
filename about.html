Product & Benefit of “goldleaf original”

Everybody knows gold leaf – but what is the special feature of goldleaf original? goldleaf original combines old craftsmanship with innovative production methods: It is also made of 24 carats genuine gold, available in different bright golden colors and joint less decors, but unlike the traditional gold leaf our new technology offers a protected and long-lasting surface without any fading! goldleaf original is abrasion resistant, easy to clean and insensitive to water. It is an excellent finishing of substrates like glass, acrylic glass, wood, marble, aluminum and gypsum board and absolutely predestined for luxury designs on mirrors, windows, doors, ceilings, walls, pieces of furniture or floor areas. Large panels as well as razor sharp contours, also fine line art or colored decors are possible. goldleaf original is self-adhesive and thus very timesaving during the installation. We offer custom made solutions which are individually manufactured under highest quality standard „made in Germany“. Every work piece is handmade, genuine and made to measure – unique work pieces for unique projects. Make the difference – feel the difference: with goldleaf original you get luxury feeling in every square metre!

The history of gold leaf

Gold leaf has almost 5.000 years of tradition. In modern gold leaf production, much has changed since the Middle Ages, but the basic principle has remained the same. The laws of physics cannot be outwitted and the special ductility of gold can only be achieved by following certain rules. The ancient recipes for the various alloys are well-kept secrets. Percentage & Certificates Only gold leaf that contains pure gold can be defined as gold leaf. Various components form an alloy which is poured into moulds, allowed to cool and then progressively thinned. Gold leaves of different carats are available on the market, but we only use 24 carats of pure genuine gold!
