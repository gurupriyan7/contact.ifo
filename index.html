goldleaf Original ®


Everybody knows gold leaf – but what is the special feature of goldleaf Original? goldleaf original combines old craftsmanship with innovative production methods: It is also made of 24 carat genuine gold, available in different bright golden colors and joint less decors, but unlike the traditional gold leaf our new technology offers a protected and long-lasting surface without any fading! goldleaf original is abrasion resistant, easy to clean and insensitive to water. It is an excellent finishing of substrates like glass, acrylic glass, wood, marble, aluminum and gypsum board and absolutely predestined for luxury designs on mirrors, windows, doors, ceilings, walls, pieces of furniture or floor areas.
Large panels as well as razor sharp contours, also fine line art or colored decors are possible.
goldleaf original is self-adhesive and thus very timesaving during the installation.
We offer custom made solutions which are individually manufactured under highest quality standard „Made in Germany“. Every work piece is handmade, genuine and made to measure – unique work pieces for unique projects.
Make the difference – feel the difference: with goldleaf original you get luxury feeling in every square metre!
